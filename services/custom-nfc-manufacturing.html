Custom NFC Manufacturing

Bespoke NFC products for unique business requirements — shapes, materials, and programming tailored to your vision.

When Standard Products Aren't Enough

Not every business fits a standard credit card or A6 standee. TapRate's custom NFC manufacturing service handles bespoke projects — from event keychain tags and wristbands to shaped acrylic displays, wooden NFC plaques, and embedded chips in existing products.

Because we manufacture in-house at our South Bopal studio, we can iterate on prototypes quickly and scale production without relying on overseas suppliers with 6-week lead times.

Custom Capabilities

  • Non-standard card shapes and sizes
  • Alternative materials: wood, acrylic, silicone, fabric
  • NFC keychains, wristbands, and lanyard tags
  • Embedded chips in existing promotional items
  • Multi-URL programming and NDEF formatting
  • Prototype development and small-batch runs
  • White-label manufacturing for agencies
