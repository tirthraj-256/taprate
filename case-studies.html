Restaurant · Satellite, Ahmedabad

Mehta's Kitchen: 23 to 187 Google Reviews in 6 Weeks

The Challenge

Mehta's Kitchen, a popular vegetarian restaurant on Satellite Road, had just 23 Google reviews despite serving 200+ customers daily. Staff verbally asked for reviews, but completion rates were under 5%.

The Solution

TapRate installed two branded Google review standees — one at the billing counter and one at the exit. Each standee was programmed to open Mehta's Kitchen Google review page with a single tap.

The Results

  • 164 new Google reviews in 6 weeks
  • Rating improved from 4.1 to 4.7 stars
  • Page 1 ranking for "best vegetarian restaurant Satellite"
  • 15% increase in walk-in traffic from Google Maps

"The standee on our counter changed everything. Customers tap while waiting for their bill." — Rajesh Mehta, Owner

Dental Clinic · Navrangpura

Kapoor Dental: Google Rating 3.8 → 4.6 in Two Months

The Challenge

Dr. Sneha Kapoor's dental clinic had satisfied patients but a 3.8-star Google rating dragged down by a handful of old negative reviews. New positive reviews were rare.

The Solution

TapRate supplied 8 Google review NFC cards — one for each staff member — programmed to Kapoor Dental's review page. Reception also received a counter standee.

The Results

  • 89 new 5-star reviews in 8 weeks
  • Overall rating rose from 3.8 to 4.6
  • New patient enquiries up 22% from Google

"Staff hand review cards to happy patients after treatment. It's natural and effective." — Dr. Sneha Kapoor
