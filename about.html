About TapRate Business Cards & Review Stands

Ahmedabad-born. Gujarat-focused. Helping businesses tap smarter since our first NFC card rolled off the press.

Our Story

From South Bopal to 40+ Cities

TapRate started with a simple observation: paper business cards end up in drawers, and asking customers to "find us on Google" rarely works. NFC technology solves both problems with one tap — and we built a local manufacturing studio in South Bopal to make it accessible for Indian businesses.

Today, TapRate Business Cards & Review Stands serves over 2,500 businesses across Gujarat and pan-India. We are not resellers importing generic products — every NFC card, review standee, and custom tag is designed, printed, programmed, and quality-checked in-house at our Ahmedabad studio.

Our team combines graphic design, NFC engineering, and customer support under one roof. That means faster turnaround, honest pricing, and the ability to walk in before you order.
